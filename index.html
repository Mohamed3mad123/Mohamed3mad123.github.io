Ice Cream Shop Performance Analysis

A comprehensive end-to-end data analysis project for an ice cream retail business (2023 - 2025). It transforms raw sales data into interactive dashboards to monitor financial performance and simulate marketing decisions using "What-if" analysis.

Management struggled to identify profitable vs best-selling products, lacked clarity on peak demand hours for staffing, and couldn't measure the direct impact of discounts on net margins.

  • Used Power Query for ETL processes and data cleaning.
  • Created a custom Calendar table for advanced Time Intelligence.
  • Developed DAX measures for KPIs: Profit Margin, AVO, and YoY growth.
  • Peak demand occurs at 11:00 PM on Fridays.
  • Cones lead in revenue ($89K), while Waffles have the highest profit margin (73%).
  • Evening shifts account for 50.5% of total sales volume.
  • Optimize staffing for evening shifts and weekends.
  • Launch "Combo" bundles (Cones & Cups) based on Market Basket Analysis.
  • Prioritize marketing for high-margin items like Waffles and Milkshakes.
