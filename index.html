Ice Cream Shop Analysis

A comprehensive end-to-end data analysis project for an ice cream retail business (2023 - 2025). It monitors financial performance and simulates marketing decisions using What-if analysis.

Management struggled to identify profitable vs best-selling products, lacked clarity on peak demand hours for staffing, and couldn't measure the direct impact of discounts on net margins.

Used Power Query for ETL, created a custom Calendar table for Time Intelligence, and developed complex DAX measures for KPIs like Profit Margin, AVO, and Year-over-Year (YoY) growth.

  • Peak demand occurs at 11:00 PM on Fridays.
  • Cones lead in revenue ($89K), while Waffles have the highest profit margin (73%).
  • Evening shifts account for 50.5% of total sales.
  • Optimize staffing for evening shifts to improve customer service.
  • Launch "Combo" bundles (Cones & Cups) based on Market Basket Analysis results.
  • Prioritize marketing for high-margin items like Waffles to boost overall profitability.
