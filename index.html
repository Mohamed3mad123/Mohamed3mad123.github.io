Beyond Charts: A Story of Business Growth & Operational Efficiency

Business Intelligence | Operational Analysis
Power BI SQL Excel

This project is not just a dashboard; it's a narrative of performance and strategic decision-making. I analyzed sales data to uncover the "why" behind the numbers, focusing on profitability and market acceptance.

Key Insights:
  • High Efficiency: Achieved an 87% net profit margin on $1.76M in total sales, reflecting excellent cost control.
  • Product Quality: Maintained an exceptionally low return rate of 0.99%, indicating high market satisfaction with the 833K units sold.
  • Growth Driver: Identified that opening 11 new branches in 1998 was a direct catalyst for increased sales and a growing customer base (8,000 new customers).
  • Target Audience: Pinpointed that customers with income <$60K and seniors are the most active and largest segments.
Strategic Recommendations:
  • Focus on North West: Reallocate marketing budget to the highest-performing region ($0.84M in revenue).
  • Tailored Loyalty Programs: Design specific offers for the large senior demographic and the <$60K income segment to maximize customer lifetime value.
  • Review Returns: Investigate the "Hermanos Red Pepper" product to safeguard its high-profit margin despite its higher return rate.

Unlocking Profitability: When Discounts Drive Real Growth (Market Basket & What-If)

Cross-Selling | Pricing Strategy | Simulation
Power BI Advanced DAX Statistical Analysis

Does a discount always reduce profit? This project proves that data-driven discounts can be a powerful growth lever. I combined Market Basket Analysis with What-If simulations to optimize cross-selling and pricing strategies.

Key Achievements:
  • Identified Bundles: Used Market Basket Analysis to discover which products customers frequently purchase together, creating clear opportunities for product bundling.
  • Simulated Outcomes: Developed a What-If Analysis model to answer critical questions: "What if we offer a discount on this bundle?" and "What if that discount increases order volume slightly?".
  • Data-Driven Pricing: Compared sales and profitability scenarios (pre and post-discount) to support pricing decisions based on actual numbers, not assumptions.
  • Business Impact: Helped the business increase Average Order Value (AOV) and reduce the risk associated with random discounting.

Strategic F&B Turnaround: Data-Driven Profits Over Simple Discounts

Business Turnaround | Profitability Analysis | Menu Engineering
Power BI Correlation Analysis Market Basket Analysis

For a business in the Food & Beverage sector, I was tasked with a clear goal: analyze declining sales and provide actionable recommendations to improve performance. This project proves that data-driven decisions reduce waste and maximize profitability.

Key Results & Strategic Moves:
  • Re-evaluating Discounts: Through Correlation Analysis, I discovered that direct discounts had a very limited effect on increasing quantity sold but a severe negative impact on profit margin. Customers purchased based on perceived value, not just price.
  • Menu Engineering: Identified that the "Waffle" achieved a high-profit margin (up to 73%) despite not being the highest-selling item. My recommendation: re-direct marketing and menu engineering efforts towards high-margin products to maximize net profit.
  • Bundle Strategy: Replaced random discounts with calculated product bundles (derived from Market Basket Analysis) to raise the Average Order Value (AOV).
  • Predictive Simulation: Concluded the project with a What-If Analysis to test different pricing scenarios and discount levels *before* implementation, predicting their exact effect on expected profits.
